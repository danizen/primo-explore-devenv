How does this work?

Scholar OneSearch provides simple, one-stop searching for books and e-books, videos, articles, digital media, and more. Search local Northeastern collections and global resources, with many items available in full text online.

Scholar OneSearch also helps you manage your research. Sign in with your myNEU credentials in order to:

  • Renew books and videos
  • Create lists
  • Export citations to Refworks and Endnote Web
  • Order materials from other libraries.
  • View full search results. (Some databases, like Web of Science, only show results when you're signed in.)
